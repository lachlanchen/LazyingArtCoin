Token Utility

The reward layer for the LazyingArt universe.

LazyingArt Coin powers incentives across OnlyIdeas, EchoMind, and the broader LazyingArt studio. Every contribution, conversation, and creative insight earns value that can move on-chain when you are ready.

Engineered for creators

The LazyingArt Coin service issues credits on contribution, settles balances in ETH or ERC-20 tokens, and keeps a transparent ledger. It connects the community spaces where creative work, AI dialogue, and membership intersect.

Designed for real utility

Credits start inside our apps and graduate to on-chain value. The MVP shipping in this repository focuses on reliability, clear conversion, and transparent accounting.

Creator Earnings

OnlyIdeas rewards community proposals, critiques, and curated research with credits that convert to LazyingArt Coin balances.

AI Collaboration

EchoMind sessions unlock bonuses for multilingual coaching, conversation hosting, and prompt design that keeps the experience human-first.

Studio Loyalty

LazyingArt flagship launches, hardware drops, and field labs issue seasonal quests tied to the coin, giving contributors early access and revenue share experiments.

On-Chain Payouts

Secure Tornado + Web3 backend pays out in ETH or a configured ERC-20 token when balances clear review, with idempotent requests and nonce locking.
